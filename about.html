About Us

Why MS Aluminium

MS Aluminium distinguished company in the field of aluminium subcontracting, Our company has been providing fabrication and installation of aluminium and glazed structures and decorative metal works to architects, engineers, contractors and developers with professional aluminium solutions with offices in UAE. We offer multiple integrated services through one company, with project management supported by our manufacturing facilities. Our services includes curtain walls, structural glazing, skylights, windows, doors, shopfronts, hand railings, wall cladding, cast aluminium grills, claustra and kitchen cabinets. We customize designs and manufacture these products according to our clients’ needs.
